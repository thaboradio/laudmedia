Laud entertainment and media corporation is a full-service entertainment and media operations organization, supporting a diverse roster of talent and production content via artist management, music publishing, touring, production, live entertainment, strategic brand development and beyond. We are 100% black owned burgeoning entity forging to become a preeminent global entertainment and media force.

LAUD ENTERTAINMENT AND MEDIA CORPORATION has formed strategic partnerships with leading industry experts in various media and entertainment disciplines dedicated to owning, producing and delivering content to mainstream audiences with targeted focus on the powerful demographic of diverse content consumers and fans. The company was forged together by a team of industry mavericks with over 45 years collective media and entertainment industry experience, who sought through the spirit of entrepreneurship to create an entity that would bridge the gap between live entertainment and modern and traditional media practices and tactics. Though the company may be relatively young by industry standards it lacks nothing when it comes to experience and the knowhow of industry operations as each of our directors have years of industry game time under their belt respectively and the battle scars to show for it.

We are privileged to work with artists, designers and content producers across all disciplines to bring their creativity to life on various platforms around the country, continent and the world through technology and more. We have built a library of marquee media properties that have established us as a trusted brand which consistently delivers high-quality, commercial entertainment and marketing products and services.
